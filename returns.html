Return & Exchange Policy

Eligible Cases

  • Within 48 hours of receipt, if items are severely damaged, incorrect, or missing, please take photos and contact us promptly.
  • Items must be unused and in original packaging (including gifts/accessories/tags).

Not Accepted

  • Non-quality issues (e.g., personal preference for taste/color/size).
  • Food or supplements once opened (for hygiene reasons).
  • Overdue requests or without valid proof/receipt.

How to Request

Contact us via WhatsApp or email with your order number, description, and clear photos. After verification, we will arrange reshipment, return, or refund. Refunds are typically made to the original payment method and depend on third-party processing time.

Shipping Fees

If the issue is on our side (e.g., wrong item/damage), we cover the shipping cost; otherwise, shipping is not covered.
