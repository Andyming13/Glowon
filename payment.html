付款方式

目前我們僅支援透過 WhatsApp 與我們聯絡以安排付款。你可先將商品加入購物車，然後在 WhatsApp 告知我們訂單內容與送貨資料，我們會回覆報價與付款指引。

立即 WhatsApp 聯絡我們

流程

  • 在網站挑選商品並記下數量／規格。
  • 透過 WhatsApp 傳送訂單內容與送貨資料。
  • 我們確認存貨與運費後，回覆報價與付款指引。
  • 完成付款並回傳截圖／參考編號，即安排出貨。

付款渠道（以回覆為準）

現階段以 WhatsApp 回覆為準，常見方式可能包括：轉數快（FPS）／銀行轉帳／PayMe 等；如有更新會以 WhatsApp 通知。

收據與退款

付款後可索取收據；如因缺貨無法出貨，我們會按原路退款（時間視第三方處理而定）。

提示：為保障安全，請只使用我們於 WhatsApp 提供的收款賬戶／連結。

Payment Methods

We currently arrange payments via WhatsApp only. Please add items to cart, then message us on WhatsApp with your order details and delivery info. We’ll reply with a quote and payment instructions.

Chat with us on WhatsApp

Flow

  • Select products and note quantities/specs.
  • Send your order and delivery info via WhatsApp.
  • We confirm stock & shipping and reply with the payment instructions.
  • Complete payment and share a proof/transaction ref; we will arrange dispatch.

Channels (subject to our reply)

Payment methods may include FPS/bank transfer/PayMe, etc. The final method will be confirmed in WhatsApp.

Receipt & Refund

Receipt is available upon request. If an order cannot be fulfilled due to stock issues, a refund will be processed to the original method (timing depends on the payment provider).

Tip: For security, please use only the account/links we provide over WhatsApp.
